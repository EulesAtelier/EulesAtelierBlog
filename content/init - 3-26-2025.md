I am creating this blog as an outlet for my ideas and criticisms of the games I have played. 

Previously, I have had verbal conversations, discord chats, eclectic notes in my journals, but I feel the need to put all these ideas down somewhere, and I needed an audience who was not captive to my ramblings (Mom, I am so sorry). I have other reasons for this as well:

1. My writing skills. I would like to get better at criticism and at writing, I have been in a slump and noticed my skills degrading.
2. Longevity and centrality, I need a place that will last and a place I can show people my work. 
3. Finally, I need a space where I can talk about game design and creation as a craft, as a way for me to grow as a game designer. 

I do believe an introduction is in order at this point. My name is Athene, I am a computer science student, and a game designer and developer. Outside of games and computers, I adore historical dress, philosophy, and literature (Ursula K. LaGuin is my current favorite author.)

Going forward, I’m going to write about the things I have played previously, and when I play new releases or games in my backlog, I will post about them too. 

Thank you for reading,
A Y
